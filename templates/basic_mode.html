How to Use - Basic Mode Gestures

  • Move Cursor: Move your index finger while keeping others down.
  • Left Click: Bring your index finger and thumb close together to pinch.
  • Right Click: Pinch with your middle finger and thumb.
  • Double Click: Tap index and thumb together twice quickly.
  • Scroll: Move both index and middle fingers up/down simultaneously.
  • Stop Cursor: Close your hand into a fist.

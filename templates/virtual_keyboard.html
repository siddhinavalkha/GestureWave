Virtual Keyboard Mode

Control your system using gesture-based typing.

How to Use

  • Step 1: Position your hand in front of your webcam.
  • Step 2: Move your index finger to hover over the virtual keyboard keys.
  • Step 3: Pinch your index and thumb together to simulate a key press.
  • Step 4: The key will be typed in your active window (e.g., Notepad).
  • Step 5: Use the on-screen textbox to search or verify keystrokes (optional).
